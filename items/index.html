Misc about SU

Each item types has one SU and one SSU (sacred-sacred-unique). SU on the left, SSU on the right. A SSU isn't alway better than an SU for your build. Read the documentation to check the item's stats and if they fit your build.

Sometimes a simple TU with good stats is better than an inadapted SU!
